Company Profile

Who We Are

M/S RDA Industries is a reliable manufacturer for Auto Parts, Tractor Parts, and Precision Parts — and a supplier of an array of Alloy Steel Products.

Our product range includes Low, Medium & High Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Free Cutting Steel, Stainless Steel, and Forging and Investment Casting. All alloy steel products are zinc-plated for protection from rust.

Our target is clearly defined: to achieve quality not only dimensionally, physically and chemically — but with outlook and packaging as per the customers' requirements, meeting all service requirements of logistics.

We look forward to a long-term business association with manufacturers for our domestic as well as export unit, supplying automobile components to both domestic and international markets.
