Quality Philosophy

Quality is Our Culture

At RDA Industries, all products are manufactured under stringent quality control methods. Quality has become integrated with our culture and the quality system used is in compliance with the best standards in the world.

Our technicians monitor the production process to ensure high quality international standards. We use the best raw material and adopt strict quality control methods, supervised by a team of most educated and technically qualified inspectors.

Quality assurance occurs at all stages of manufacturing — right from raw material inspection through to the final alloy steel product — ensuring every dimension, physical property and chemical composition meets specification.
